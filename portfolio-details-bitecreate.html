During my internship at Bit Create Company from July 2022 to December 2022, I was assigned to create a simple food website showcasing Filipino dishes, complete with content descriptions and seller information. As part of the project, I utilized my web development skills to design and build the website's user interface, ensuring a seamless and visually appealing experience for the website's visitors. The website not only provided an online platform for Filipino food enthusiasts to learn about various dishes but also supported local businesses by featuring information about the sellers of the food products. Through this project, I was able to gain valuable experience in web development, project management, and client communication.
