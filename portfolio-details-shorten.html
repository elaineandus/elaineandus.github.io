ShortenLink is a free URL shortener website developed by Elaine S. Andus. Since the dawn of the internet, links have been the way to get from one place to another online. Think about it, you either start by searching for something and then clicking a link, or by typing a link directly into your browser’s address bar. There’s no other way to get around. So, this website was made involving everything to do with links. Its purpose is simply to compress, shrink or shorten any link you will input.
